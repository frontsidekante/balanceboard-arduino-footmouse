# Build your own footmouse

## 1. Parts
* 1 Balanceboard
* 1 Arduino Leonardo
* 1 MPU6050 Breakout Board
* 2 TCRT5000 IR Sensors
* 2 LEDs
* 2 small proto boards
* 6 resistors (2x 220Ω, 2x 100Ω, 2x 47Ω)
* 14 jumper wires
* Breadboard or additional proto board for power distribution

## 2. Tools
* Soldering Iron
* Hot melt gun
